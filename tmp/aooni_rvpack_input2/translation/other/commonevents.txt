\>\c[2]PLACES OF INTEREST\c[0]\<\#Despite almost being destroyed by a fire over fifty\#years ago, smoke seems to constantly billow inside\#the building, making seeing and breathing difficult.
The Inbetween
The Inbetween is a dimension between dimensions, a\#spiritual plane of exsistance that connects to all things,\#worlds, realities, life and even death.
Through it, a being is able to replace their mind with\#that of another and can be extemely dangerous, so a\#strong mind is a must.
The world of The Inbetween will always change its\#appearance based on the one who casted the spell,\#with trials and challenges being presented based\#on the casters personal fears and thoughts.
The Sentinelese believe that weak minds can not\#complete the ritual, and so they keep the ability\#to enter The Inbetween a secret, as they believe\#it to be a right of passage for adulthood.
Oni
The Oni is a supernatural being known as a YÅkai\#that was summoned from a different dimension by\#the Warden in the belief that it could help 'punish\#the sinners'
Once an Oni is summoned, the spell used will force\#the creature into a mindless rage, with only one goal,\#to protect an area - at any cost.
The Oni will place a curse on the area in order to\#protect it, which traps all who are inside, or those\#who decide to wander in. Areas damaged before\#it casts its curse will weaken its magic.
The Oni has the ability of mimicry, is immortal and\#ageless, has immense strength and is said to have\#psychic powers, able to communicate telepathically\#and also influence weak minds.
The Oni's bite also has the ability to rewrite a victims\#DNA in mere seconds, changing their form entirely into\#another Oni. Some victims may keep their looks or\#traits if their will to live is strong enough.
Slug Oni
The Slug Oni seems to be a garden variety slug that\#was mutated when infected by the Oni's DNA. Now a\#hulking beast, the Slug Oni is composed of many of its\#killed victims, which it adds to its mass via assimilation.
The Slug Oni is extremely heavy and prefers to use\#stealth as a way to surprise potential victims, slowly\#climbing on roofs and walls before hurling its body,\#crushing and assimulating itself into a larger mass.
Roach Oni
The Roach Oni is a cockroach that has been infected\#and mutated by the Oni's DNA. Unlike most other Oni\#creatures, the Roach Oni seems to have kept its baser\#instincts and memory, behaving exactly like a cockroach.
While not dangerous by itself, if provoked, or hungry\#enough, a Roach Oni will attack, often in a group of\#other Roach Oni.
Blockman Oni
The Blockman Oni is an Oni that somehow emerged\#when the original Oni was summoned. Unlike the Oni,\#Blockman Oni is a purely predatorial being, with no mind\#or thought to itself except to hunt and eat.
The Blockman Oni prefers to charge at its victims, biting\#down with its razor sharp teeth. This Oni pursues its\#victims for much longer and can become paper thin\#to fit inside otherwise impossible areas.
The Possessed
The Possessed are victims of the Oni's attempts at\#controlling people in order to achieve its goals.\#While useful to the Oni at first, The Possessed soon\#succumb to the same rage curse that affects itself.
In order to control a victim, the Oni will use its telepathic\#powers to find a weakness in a victim, drawing out an\#emotion it can use, much like flipping a switch, such as\#turning love into lust, or greed into obsession.
The Possessed are usually unaware that they are\#being manipulated until it's too late, and often think\#that their actions are their own. Once released from\#the Oni's control, the victim forgets what happened.
